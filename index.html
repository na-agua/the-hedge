About this place

Who writes here

I am a solitary, eclectic witch. I practice at the hedge — the threshold between the ordinary world and whatever lies just beyond it. I work with the moon, with shadow, with dream, with the body as a sacred instrument.

I write here for myself. To structure what I believe. To have somewhere to return to when I need to practice. If you found this and it serves you — good. Take what you need.

The hedge does not belong to anyone. It belongs to those who are willing to cross it.
