Morning 15 min
Minimum · 15 min
  1. 1
    Land 1 min
    Feet on the floor. Name the moon phase and your cycle phase aloud. One slow breath. You are here, in this body, on this Earth.
  2. 2
    Anapana 10 min
    Breath at the nostrils. Nothing else. When the mind wanders, return without judgment. This is enough. Showing up matters more than depth.
  3. 3
    One intention aloud 1 min
    One sentence. A quality of being, not a to-do. "Today I move at the pace of what I am carrying."
  4. 4
    Pachamama breath 3 min
    Breathe at her pace. Slower than feels comfortable. Feel the ground beneath you holding your weight. She is already here.
Good · 35 min
  1. 1
    Land 2 min
    Feet on floor. Moon phase, cycle phase, one breath. Set the space with intention before anything else.
  2. 2
    LBRP 10 min
    At your level the LBRP can be replaced by the Intent Shield — one breath, one clear felt intention: I am clear, I am protected, only what serves my highest good may approach. Feel it as a sphere of light around your body. Use the full LBRP before hedge riding and ceremony only. For daily practice, the shield is enough.
  3. 3
    Middle Pillar 5 min
    Crown to earth. Five centers: Kether (crown) → Daath (throat) → Tiphareth (heart) → Yesod (belly) → Malkuth (feet into earth). At each one: see the light, vibrate the divine name, feel it expand. This travels with you into the world. It is your personal armour. 3 minutes is enough when practiced daily.
  4. 4
    Anapana into open awareness 15 min
    Begin at the nostrils (Anapana) for 10 minutes, then release the anchor and rest in open awareness for 5. Let the mind be sky, not wall.
  5. 5
    Intention aligned to the moon 3 min
    One sentence that responds to today's moon phase energy. See the moon guidance below. Speak it aloud. Let your voice carry it.
Ideal · 60 min
  1. 1
    Land 2 min
    Full landing. Moon, cycle, body, breath. Feel the weight of the day ahead with equanimity.
  2. 2
    LBRP 10 min
    Full LBRP with all gestures, vibrations, and archangel visualizations — reserve this for before hedge riding, ceremony, or when the space genuinely needs clearing. In daily life: use the Pachamama field — one breath at her pace, feel her presence extend around you as protection. Her field works at a level the LBRP approximates.
  3. 3
    Middle Pillar 5 min
    Full Middle Pillar — slow, deliberate, each center held. Then the Circulation of Light: feel the energy circulating through the whole body and aura. This is your most complete daily protection and energization in one practice.
  4. 4
    Pranayama — alternate nostril 8 min
    4 rounds of Nadi Shodhana. Balances the hemispheres, prepares the body for deep sitting. Left nostril first — lunar, receiving.
  5. 5
    Vipassana body scan 25 min
    Crown to feet, feet to crown. Equanimity with all sensations — pleasant and unpleasant equally. This is the core of the Ideal morning. Everything else prepares for this.
  6. 6
    Metta 8 min
    Self first — always self first. Then those you love. Then neutral beings. Then difficult ones. Then all beings on Earth. Then all universal beings. You know how far this can go.
  7. 7
    Intention + Pachamama 2 min
    State your intention. Then sit one minute at her pace. Let her confirm it or quietly correct it.

Midday 2 min
Minimum · 2 min
  1. 1
    Stop 2 min
    Three conscious breaths. One question asked honestly: am I still in my body? That's all. Return to what you were doing.
Good · 10 min
  1. 1
    Anapana sit 7 min
    A proper pause in the middle of the day. Breath at the nostrils. Let the morning settle. The midday sit catches what the morning missed.
  2. 2
    Realignment 3 min
    Check the moon phase energy. Ask: am I still living from my morning intention, or did I lose it somewhere around 10am? One sentence to reorient.
Ideal · 20 min
  1. 1
    Yoga nidra 15 min
    Lying down. Full body relaxation. Set a Sankalpa — a seed intention — at the beginning and end. The midday nidra is one of the most restorative practices available. 15 minutes equals 2 hours of sleep in nervous system benefit.
  2. 2
    Intention check + Metta pulse 5 min
    Brief loving-kindness for whoever you have encountered this morning. Not elaborate — one breath of goodwill per person. This is the curandera's midday practice: send what you have received forward.
🕯️
Evening 10 min
Minimum · 10 min
  1. 1
    Three sentences 3 min
    What happened today — outer and inner. Three sentences only. Not a journal. A witness statement.
  2. 2
    Release 2 min
    Name one thing you are putting down before sleep. Say it aloud. One exhale. Let it go physically — a gesture, a breath, a relaxing of the hands.
  3. 3
    Trataka 5 min
    Candle flame. Soft gaze. Let the day dissolve into the light. Don't blink until the eyes water naturally. Then close them and hold the after-image.
Good · 25 min
  1. 1
    Vipassana body scan 15 min
    Where did the day land in the body? Scan slowly. Notice what is held, what is tight, what is unresolved. The body keeps the score of the day. This practice reads it honestly.
  2. 2
    Shadow check 5 min
    What triggered me today? What was the hidden gift in it? One minute per question. No bypassing. The shadow named at night does not visit in dreams uninvited.
  3. 3
    Dream invitation to Pachamama 3 min
    Before sleep, ask her to sit with you in the dream space. Set one question for the night. The dreaming hedge witch asks before she crosses.
  4. 4
    Three gratitudes 2 min
    Specific. Not "I am grateful for my health" but "I am grateful for the warmth of the cup in my hands this morning." Specificity is what makes gratitude land.
Ideal · 45 min
  1. 1
    Pranayama or holotropic breathwork 15 min
    Choose based on what the day needs to discharge. Pranayama for balance and clarity. Holotropic for deeper emotional release. Let the breath do what the mind cannot.
  2. 2
    Full Vipassana sit 20 min
    Complete body scan, crown to feet and back. Full equanimity practice. This is the evening's core. The day is digested here, in the body, not in the mind.
  3. 3
    Shadow journal 5 min
    One page. What arose today that needs witnessing. No fixing. Write it and close the notebook. The act of writing is the release.
  4. 4
    Dream intention + hedge riding invitation 3 min
    If the veil feels thin — waning moon, dark moon, Samhain season — invite a conscious crossing in the dream space. Set your intention. Ask Pachamama to accompany you. The hedge witch who has prepared can ride in sleep.
  5. 5
    Three gratitudes spoken aloud 2 min
    To the room. To Pachamama. To the day itself. Spoken, not thought. The voice in the physical world carries authority in the spirit world.

Practice Guidelines

✦ Minimum is not failure. Showing up at minimum every day is infinitely more powerful than doing ideal three times a week. Consistency is the practice.
✦ At your level, protection is intention embodied. Use the Intent Shield or Pachamama field daily. Reserve the full LBRP for hedge riding, ceremony, and clearing a space. The Middle Pillar is your essential daily practice — it both protects and energizes.
✦ Each time of day is independent. You can do Minimum morning, Ideal midday, Good evening. There is no failing the whole day because one anchor was minimal.
✦ The body scan evening practice digests the day. What you don't process in the body before sleep visits you in dreams — often as shadow, sometimes as message. The scan makes the difference.
✦ Pachamama is available at any level. Even on a Minimum day, her breath is there. She does not require elaborate preparation. She requires slowness.
✦ Speak aloud. Intentions, gratitudes, releases — your voice in the physical world carries authority in the spirit world. Do not only think them.
✦ Write immediately after dreaming. Even one sentence before you move. The hedge crosses in sleep — the journal is the harvest.
Microdosing · Lunar Protocol Aligned to the moon cycle, not a fixed schedule
🌑New Moon
Begin a series. Plant the intention for this cycle on the first microdose. What is this medicine helping you grow? Name it before you take it. The new moon + medicine combination is potent for setting the direction of an entire cycle.
🌒Waxing
Active days. Pair with Good or Ideal morning practice. The waxing energy amplifies what is growing — creativity, focus, momentum. Let the medicine serve the intention you planted at the new moon.
🌕Full Moon
Pause the medicine. Let the peak be natural. The full moon already opens what needs opening — the medicine and the moon together can amplify more than you can integrate. Trust the moon to do its work alone.
🌖Waning
Shadow orientation. If continuing to microdose, shift the intention toward what needs releasing. What did the full moon illuminate that now needs to go? Waning medicine goes inward, downward, into what is hidden.
🌑Dark Moon
Rest. No medicine. Let the system empty completely. The dark moon is the fallow time — for the body and the psyche both. What you don't allow to empty cannot fill again cleanly.
