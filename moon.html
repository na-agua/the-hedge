The Moon & the Hedge Witch

For the hedge witch, the moon is not decoration. It is the primary timer of your practice — more reliable than any calendar, more honest than any clock. The moon governs when the veil thins, when spirits move more freely, when your own psychic sensitivity peaks.

New and dark moons: The veil is thin from within. Your own unconscious opens. Best for shadow work, deep dreaming, setting intentions that come from the quiet rather than the noise.

Full moon: The veil is thin from without. Spirits, Fae, and ancestral energies move more freely. The most potent night for hedge riding — and the most important night to be protected before you cross.

Waning phases: Releasing, banishing, cutting. If something needs to end — a pattern, a relationship, a belief you've outgrown — work with the waning moon. It is already pulling things away.

Liminal moon moments: The exact moment of phase change — new to waxing, full to waning — is a threshold in time. A crossing. The hedge witch feels these shifts in the body before knowing them with the mind.

Simple ritual guidance

How to Work Each Phase

🌑 New Moon — The Planting

Sit in darkness before lighting anything. Let your eyes adjust. In that dark, speak your intention aloud — once, clearly, as a declaration not a wish. Light a single candle after. The flame is the intention made visible. Write one sentence in your notebook: what you are calling in, not how.

🌕 Full Moon — The Peak

Do the LBRP first — always. Then sit with two objects: one that represents what you are receiving, one that represents what you are releasing. Hold the receiving object to your heart. Hold the releasing object loosely in your open palm. When ready, set the releasing object down — outside if possible, at the base of a tree or stone. Leave it. Don't take it back.

🌑→🌕 Waxing — Building the Work

Each night of the waxing moon, do one small act in the direction of your intention. Not a ritual — an act. Send the message. Make the appointment. Write the page. Magic that doesn't move in the physical world stays only in the mind.

🌕→🌑 Waning — The Releasing

Write what you are releasing on paper. Be specific — not 'anxiety' but 'the belief that I am too much for people to handle.' Burn it on the last quarter or dark moon. Watch the smoke carry it. Don't journal about it after. Let it be gone.

🌘 Dark Moon — Before the New

Rest. Do nothing magical. This is the fallow phase — the soil between harvests. The hedge witch who cannot rest cannot ride. Sleep more. Dream consciously. Let the cycle empty completely before it fills again.
